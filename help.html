How to Use TaskMaster Pro

1

Create Custom Lists

Go to "Create List" to make custom categories for your tasks. Lists sync across all your devices when logged in.

2

Add Tasks

Click on any list in "Your Lists" to add tasks with due dates and reminder times. Set reminders from minutes to years in advance.

3

Enable Notifications

Allow notifications when prompted to get reminders for your tasks. Works on iOS 16.4+, Android, and desktop browsers.

4

Create an Account

Sign up to sync your lists and tasks across all devices. Your data is securely stored in the cloud.

5

Customize Voice

Visit "Options" to choose your preferred voice and speech rate for voice announcements.

6

Install as App

On mobile: Add to Home Screen. On desktop: Install from browser menu. Works like a native app!
