Getting Started

üìù Creating Your First List

  1. Click on "Create List" in the navigation menu
  2. Enter a name for your list (e.g., "Work Tasks", "Shopping", "Goals")
  3. Add an optional description and choose a color
  4. Click "Create List" to save it

‚úÖ Adding Tasks

  1. Go to "Your Lists"
  2. Click on the list where you want to add a task
  3. Fill in the task title and optional reminder message
  4. Set the due date and time
  5. Choose when to be reminded (at due time, or minutes/days/weeks before)
  6. Click "Add Task"

üîî Enabling Notifications

On iOS and Android, the app will automatically ask for notification permission when you first visit. Click "Allow" to receive task reminders even when the app is closed.

iOS users: For best experience, add the app to your Home Screen by tapping the Share button and selecting "Add to Home Screen".

  • With account: All data syncs to the cloud automatically
  • Guest mode: Data stored locally, temporary (2 hour limit)
