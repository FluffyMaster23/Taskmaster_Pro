Welcome to Taskmaster Pro

Welcome to Taskmaster Pro, a todo app to stay productive and on top of your tasks.

With this app you can create unlimited lists and tasks, set reminders to years in advance and everything is on IOS and your PC.

In the following sections, all features of this app are explained in detail. These features include:

  • add tasks with due date, a reminder message(if using a voice from the "choose voice menu"
  • get reminded on the due date, even if a reminder was set for 5 minutes before, 2 days before, etc.
  • notifies you about upcoming tasks as they are due and when a reminder was set both on PC and ios.
  • requires an account if the tasks should be synced both on your PC and your phone
  • Converts time zones automatically, so the date and time of due tasks display in your current time zone, no matter where you are located!
  • Customize voice and speech settings for reminder messages, choosing from voices installed on your system. (if no voice is selected, the task will be announced and when it is due, on windows only)
  • Upcoming tasks are displayed above your task lists, as well when they are overdue.
